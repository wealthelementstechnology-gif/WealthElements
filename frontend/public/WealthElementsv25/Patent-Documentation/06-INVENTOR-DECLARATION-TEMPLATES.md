## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **File electronically** via [www.ipindia.gov.in](https://www.ipindia.gov.in) for faster processing

2. **Digital signatures** are required for online filing

3. **Priority date** is the date of filing the complete specification

4. **Publication** occurs 18 months after filing (or earlier if requested)

5. **Examination** must be requested within 48 months of filing

6. **Grant** typically takes 3-5 years from filing date

7. **Annual maintenance fees** required after grant to keep patent in force
