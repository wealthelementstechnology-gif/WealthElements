### Feature 2: Mutual Fund Analyzer

**What It Does:**
Analyzes 1,500+ mutual funds using 47+ performance metrics and recommends the top 10 funds for your specific goals.

**The 47+ Metrics Include:**
- Returns: 1Y, 3Y, 5Y, 10Y, since inception
- Risk metrics: Standard deviation, Sharpe ratio, Sortino ratio, Beta, Alpha
- Consistency: Rolling returns, downside capture, upside capture
- Expense analysis: Expense ratio vs. category average
- Fund manager tenure and track record
- AUM trends and fund stability
- Category rankings and quartile analysis

**Smart Recommendations:**
- **Goal-Based Filtering:** Different funds for short-term vs. long-term goals
- **Risk Profile Matching:** Conservative, moderate, or aggressive investor
- **Tax Efficiency:** ELSS for tax saving, debt funds for lower brackets
- **Comparison Tools:** Side-by-side analysis of up to 5 funds

**User Benefit:**
"Stop gambling with fund selection. Invest in proven winners backed by data, not marketing."
